A gas line leak may have caused a chemical plant explosion.  Natural
gas entered a house through sewer lines and exploded, ignited by a
pilot light.  Explosions two days apart leveled nearby houses fed by
separate gas lines.  Natural or propane gas caused an apartment
building explosion.  Equipment malfunction caused solar power plant
gas-fired heaters to explode.  A gas leak in a motel laundry area
caused an explosion.  Gas escaping after a well explosion contained
poisonous hydrogen sulfide.  Repairmen cutting a gas pipeline at an
offshore platform caused an explosion.  An apartment building exploded
as authorities turned off the gas at one nearby, fearing a leak.
